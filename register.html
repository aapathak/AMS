About Us

Our Mission

Institute of Engineering and Technology is an institution of higher learning dedicated to global enrichment and meeting the evolving needs of our diverse communities.
With an emphasis on innovative programs, dynamic curriculum and general education skills, we are committed to being a pioneer in the field of career-focused education.

Pledge of Public Accountability

We empower our students, faculty and staff to exceed the expectations of society through academic excellence, community enrichment and service to the public good.
As an institution of higher learning with a history of success dating back to 1900, Insitute of Engineering and Technology understands the level of commitment required to earn a degree.
To help you make an informed decision about where to earn your degree, Insitute of Engineering and Technology signed a Pledge of Accountability to provide our prospective, current, and former students with relevant, accurate, and straightforward information.
